Welcome to MemoFarma!

This document will help you to learn how to use the program (touch the icons or move the page to right or left to change the context):
  1. Start by entering the drugs list you take during the day from "Therapy";

  2. MemoFarma will remind you to take a drug activating the alarm. The ringtone will continue until you confirm this on the outcome of administration. You can indicate whether the therapy was administered or not choosing between "Taken" or "Will not take" or decide to postpone choosing "Repeat";

  3. In the Agenda the cell of today will be coloured green if all the administrations are recorded as "taken" or red if one have been recorded as "will not take". The day won't have a colour if you don't have recorded your administration.

Now we discuss the details of the following areas.
Agenda
In the Agenda you can check if you are following your therapy regularly by the days' colour. It will be green or red in case of right or wrong admistration of the therapy. It will be no coloured if you didn't indicate your administration or if the date of the day is previous the recording of the medicines'list.

You should see in a detailed way the evolution of the day touching every single day of a month, or the one of the month touching its name.

From the application's menu you can create a spreadsheet or a document with the evolution of the month you can send by email.

To change month you can use the buttons on each side of the current's month name or move the page up or down.

Therapy
Add a new medicine touching the icon "+".

Pay attention to write correctly the name, the dose and hour you must take your medicine and remember to touch on save. If the therapy has a provisional duration you also can record the final date activing the recording by the button "End date: OFF".

Modify or cancel the informations about the medicine touching the corresponding line.

Blood pressure
Add a new measuring of the pressure touching the icon "+".

You can indicate if you measured it by your own equipment or if another person did it for you (for example your doctor or a chemist).

Modify or cancel the informations touching the corresponding line.

From the menu you can create a spreadsheet or a document you can send by email with a list of the recorded measurings.

Blood sugar
Add a new measuring of the blood sugar touching the icon "+".

Modify or cancel the informations touching the corresponding line.

From the menu you can create a spreadsheet or a document you can send by email with a list of the recorded measurings.
